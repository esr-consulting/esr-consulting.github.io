About us

ESR Consulting provides engineering services specialising in the assurance of safety and reliability,
systems engineering, and training in the area of high-integrity engineered systems.

We have expertise in the interpretation and application of key Australian and international risk and
safety management standards, including AS 31000, IEC 61508, EN 50126, EN 50128, EN 50129,
and Workplace Health and Safety (WHS), offshore petroleum (OPGGS), Major Hazard Facility (MHF)
legislation and regulations, in addition to experience of the respective regulatory environments.

Services

Risk Engineering

ESR Consulting provides services in risk engineering and management. We have performed risk identification, qualitative and quantitative analysis and assessments for offshore facility operators and operators and suppliers in the rail industry.

Safety Engineering

ESR Consulting supports operators and suppliers in planning and executing their system safety programs, including safety case development, feasibility studies, and review and assessment of client Safety Management Systems.

Systems Engineering

ESR Consulting helps organisations with all aspects of their systems engineering programs, including system concept development, system definition, planning and requirements engineering, verification and validation.

Reliability Engineering

ESR Consulting provides services in Reliability, Availability and Maintainability (RAM) engineering and management. We have developed tailored RAM strategies and plans, and performed reliability modelling and analysis for operators and suppliers in the rail industry.
